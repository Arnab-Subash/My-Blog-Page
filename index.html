places must visit

Pragjyotika Senior Secondary School

Its my school and i always go there.

Pragjyotika Junior College situated in the heart of Titabar is one of the premier institutes of the state imparting education at secondary level. This is where i study . If you want to stay in jorhat for few years you can definitely take addmission in it. Or else you can just visit the place and experience the environment.



Thengal Museum

A beautiful museum about Thengal Kacharis'

A very good place to spend some time with family and friends. Here, one can see the typical things related to Assamese culture in the museum. And also children and even adults can enjoy in the gaming section in the ground floor.



Thengal Manor

A luxurius property of Hemen Baruah , Available for visits.

This being a heritage property. The old world charm has been kept intact. The lawns are mesmerising as you drive into the porch. Warm and vintage allure. Homely and traditional food was delicious. Warm and helpful staff. There is a small pool outside in the lawn which adds to the charm and delight for all travellers
